## Command Lower and Recover Boat

### Command Lower Boat

- **Bridge Boat Deck:** Boat deck party close up, ready for command lower boat, sir.
- **Request Permission:** Hoist away boat, sir.
- **Avast!**
- **Request Permission:** Swing davit boat outboard, sir. (Check aft guy heaving fore guy)
- **Avast!**
- **Request Permission:** Lower boat to deck level, sir.
- **Avast!**
- **Request Permission:** Lower boat to water line, sir.
- **Coxn Boat in Boat:** Engine started, boat ready to slip, sir.

### Command Recover Boat

- **Bridge Boat Deck:** Boat deck party close up, ready for command recover boat, sir.
- **Request Permission:** Hoist up boat to deck level, sir.
- **Avast!**
- **Request Permission:** Hoist away boat, sir.
- **Avast!**
- **Request Permission:** Swing davit boat onboard, sir.
- **Avast!**
- **Request Permission:** Lower boat to graddle, sir.
- **Avast!**
- **Boat Secure for Sea:** Permission boat party secure and fall out.
